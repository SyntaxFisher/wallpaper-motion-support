# Wallpaper Motion Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Wallpaper Motion does not require an account and does not include advertising, tracking, or analytics operated by the developer.

## Information stored on your Mac

Imported videos, downloaded wallpapers, thumbnails, favorites, settings, and the selected wallpaper are stored locally on your Mac. Wallpaper Motion does not upload imported videos or the contents of your local library.

## Pixabay browsing

Wallpaper Motion uses Pixabay to provide its Browse library. When you open Browse, search for a wallpaper, view a thumbnail, or download a wallpaper, your Mac connects directly to Pixabay. Pixabay receives the search terms and request information needed to return content, which can include your IP address and information about the requested videos.

Pixabay may process search and usage information for service operation, analytics, personalization, and service improvement. Its practices are described in the [Pixabay Privacy Policy](https://pixabay.com/service/privacy/).

You can use imported local videos without opening Browse.

## Sharing and tracking

The developer does not receive or sell personal information from Wallpaper Motion. Wallpaper Motion does not use information to track you across apps or websites.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when Wallpaper Motion or its connected services change. The latest version will remain available in this repository.

## Contact

Questions and support requests can be submitted through the [Wallpaper Motion support repository](https://github.com/SyntaxFisher/wallpaper-motion-support/issues).
